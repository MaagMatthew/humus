Easy Homemade Hummus

2 garlic cloves, mashed and roughly chopped 2 15-oz cans of chickpeas (garbanzo beans), drained 2/3 cup of tahini (roasted, not raw) 1/4 cup freshly squeezed lemon juice 1/2 cup water 1/4 cup olive oil (plus more for garnishing) 1/2 teaspoon of salt Garnishes: A sprinkling of paprika, a swirl of olive oil, toasted pine nuts, and/or chopped parsley
